| T-04-02 | Repudiation | No automated test baseline yet | accept | Phase 1 validation plan (D-06) uses visual comparison + CSV spot-check as the validation method for this phase; automated NT8 backtesting not available until Phase 9 |

| T-04-04 | Information Disclosure | No new capabilities added | accept | Pure refactor; no new attack surface |
</threat_model>

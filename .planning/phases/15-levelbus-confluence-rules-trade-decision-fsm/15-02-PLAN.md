<objective>
Wire narrative signals (absorption, exhaustion, momentum, rejection) into the LevelBus. Today narrative classifications are computed in `deep6/engines/narrative.py` and consumed by the scorer, but the zones they imply (e.g., the full-wick absorption zone) are never registered as persistent Levels — so subsequent bars can't test confluence against them. Plan 15-02 adds that insertion at `deep6/engines/vp_context_engine.py:78-112` (D-31), applies the strength ≥ 0.4 threshold (D-06), enforces the full-wick geometry (D-07), and implements cross-session decay (D-08).

Purpose: Confluence rules (plan 15-03) and the FSM (plan 15-04) both require that an absorption at bar N is still queryable as a Level at bar N+K. This plan makes that possible. Runs SEQUENTIALLY after 15-01 (Wave 2). Corrected from prior draft: 15-02 imports LevelFactory + LevelBus directly from 15-01's outputs, so parallel execution is not possible. 15-01 must land first.
Output: Modified vp_context_engine.py with narrative-Level insertion + session carry-over; narrative.py exposes structured signal accessors if needed for the factory; tests covering strength filter, wick geometry end-to-end, and cross-session decay.
</objective>

The 3-Tier Signal Clarity Hierarchy

▶ TIER 1 — HIGH PROBABILITY (TYPE_A)

Visual treatment:

  • 22px pulsing cyan (long) / magenta (short) arrow
  • Three full-width horizontal lines: cyan/mag entry, amber dashed stop, green dashed target
  • Floating BUY/SELL callout card with entry/stop/target/R:R/CONF
  • Glowing border (16px outer cyan/magenta glow)
  • Right panel ACTIVE SIGNAL section lights up + pulses
  • Optional sound + toast

Reading: "TAKE THIS TRADE NOW. Here's exactly where to enter, stop, and target."

△ TIER 2 — WATCHING (TYPE_B)

Visual treatment:

  • 10px hollow triangle, neutral gray
  • 4px tiny "B-WATCH" / "S-WATCH" label below/above
  • No entry/stop/target lines
  • No callout card
  • No sound
  • Right panel: counts in signal section but doesn't surface

Reading: "Something is happening. Could escalate to TIER 1 — keep an eye on it."

· TIER 3 — INFORMATIONAL (TYPE_C)

Visual treatment:

  • 4px tiny dim dot
  • Hidden by default (toggle in panel)
  • No label, no lines, no card, no sound
  • Right panel: counted in signal totals, hidden in feed

Reading: "Background noise. Don't act on this. Useful for retro analysis only."

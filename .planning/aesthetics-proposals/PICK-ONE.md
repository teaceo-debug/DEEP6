# DEEP6 — Pick One Aesthetic

5 minimalist designs. Reply with the letter.

---

## A — AIRPORT PICTOGRAM

3 huge geometric shapes (wall, flame, staircase) center-chart. Amber + grey. Shape carries the signal type.

```
    ▰▰▰▰▰▰      ▲           ▐█
    ▰▰▰▰▰▰     ▲ ▲            ▐█
    ▼ ▼ ▼     ▲ ▲ ▲             ▐█
   ▼ ▼ ▼ ▼   ▲     ▲              ▐█

      1 5 8 4 2 . 2 5
```

- **1 color (amber)** + grey
- Tells you WHICH signal (abs/exh/imb) fired
- Big — 120px symbols

---

## B — TYPOGRAPHY ONLY

ONE giant word + price. That's it.

```
                                    
                                    
              BUY                   
            18452.25                
                                    
                                    
```

- **Cyan** for BUY · **magenta** for SELL · **grey** for WAIT
- 120pt type
- Reads in 80ms from across the desk
- Black void everywhere else

---

## C — FIGHTER JET HUD

Phosphor green vector lines. Reticle, diamond, brackets. Looks like an F-16 HUD.

```
   ┌─        ─┐
   │          │     ◇  ← target
   │    ⊕     │     ⊕ ← current price
   │          │
   └─        ─┘
   STATE: BUY  PRICE: 18247.75
```

- **One color (phosphor green `#00FF41`)**
- Pure line-art
- Most "institutional" feel

---

## D — STOPLIGHT (3 BIG CIRCLES)

3 huge circles right edge. ONE lights up. Done.

```
                              ○ ← SELL (off)
                              
                              ○ ← WAIT (off)
                              
                             ●● ← BUY (lit, cyan-green)
                          18247.25
```

- 128px circles, full saturation when lit
- Universally readable in 200ms
- Most unambiguous design ever made

---

## E — JAPANESE ZEN (single stroke)

One vertical line. One number. Pure black void.

```
                                    
                                    
                ┃                   
                ┃    1 7 2 4 5 . 2 5
                ┃        (cyan)
                ┃                   
                                    
```

- Cyan stroke (BUY) or magenta stroke (SELL)
- 12×240px sumi-ink line
- 98% empty space
- Hover shows stop/target
